## 📝 Module Summaries

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
Created a one-page visual infographic using Canva covering what digital literacy means, safe internet practices, and professional online presence. The infographic is designed for first-year students to quickly grasp key digital skills.

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Set up professional profiles on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. Each platform serves a different purpose — GitHub for code, LinkedIn for career networking, and Kaggle for data science practice. Screenshots of all profiles are included in the task-2-portfolio/ folder.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
Completed a beginner challenge on HackerRank and built a 5-question Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz using Google Forms for use with batchmates.

🔗 **Google Form Quiz Link:** https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdKV2uAN1BoLGeZjFVyzJ16-JGKFaFJyhT5gtPl282ZuySqyQ/viewform

### Task 4 – Email Etiquette
Drafted two professional emails — one requesting a deadline extension from a professor and another expressing internship interest to a company coordinator. Also created a Social Media Do's and Don'ts checklist.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Researched and wrote a case study on Identity Theft, covering how it happens, who is targeted, and consequences. Also created a prevention checklist with 8+ actionable tips including UPI safety and reporting via cybercrime.gov.in and Helpline 1930.
