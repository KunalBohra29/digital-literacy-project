## What the Infographic Shows
The infographic is titled **"Be Smart Online: A Digital Literacy Guide for Students"** and covers the following three topics:

1. **What is Digital Literacy?**
   - The ability to find, evaluate, create, and communicate information using digital technologies.
   - Why it matters for college students in today's world.

2. **Safe Internet Practices**
   - Use strong, unique passwords for every account.
   - Never share OTPs, PINs, or passwords with anyone.
   - Always check URLs before clicking links.
   - Use only secure (HTTPS) websites for transactions.

3. **Professional Online Presence**
   - Keep your LinkedIn and GitHub profiles updated.
   - Think before you post — your digital footprint is permanent.
   - Use your college email for academic and professional sign-ups.
