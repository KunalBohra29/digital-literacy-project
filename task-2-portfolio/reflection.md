# Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio

## Platforms Set Up

### 1. GitHub (github.com)
GitHub is a platform for hosting and sharing code. It is widely used by developers, students, and researchers to collaborate on projects and showcase their work. I created a profile README repository that introduces me, my branch, and what I hope to learn.

### 2. LinkedIn (linkedin.com)
LinkedIn is a professional networking platform used by employers, recruiters, and professionals worldwide. I filled in my Education section with my current degree (B.Tech CSE-AI), institution (VIT Bhopal University), and expected graduation year (2029).

### 3. Kaggle (kaggle.com)
Kaggle is a platform for data science and machine learning competitions, datasets, and learning. It is used by students and professionals to practice ML skills and build a data science portfolio.

Over the next four years, I plan to use GitHub to host all my academic and personal projects, making them accessible to potential employers. LinkedIn will help me connect with professionals in the AI and tech industry and discover internship opportunities. Kaggle will be my go-to platform for practicing machine learning concepts taught in class and participating in beginner-level competitions. Together, these three platforms will form the foundation of my professional digital identity by the time I graduate in 2029.
