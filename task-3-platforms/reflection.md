## Reflection
HackerRank introduced me to the structured environment of competitive programming, where even the simplest problem teaches you to think logically and read instructions carefully. Google Forms, on the other hand, showed me how easy it is to collect and analyse data digitally — a skill that will be incredibly useful for surveys, feedback collection, and academic research throughout my degree.
